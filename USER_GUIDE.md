# Fincred Gift Exchange Portal - User Guide

## Overview
This portal is designed to manage the Fincred Finance internal gift exchange. It allows administrators to set up events, add colleagues with their preferences, and automatically assign Secret Santas.

## 1. Admin Instructions (Organizing an Event)

### Step 1: Access the Dashboard
- Navigate to the **Admin Dashboard** by clicking the "Admin Portal" button on the home page or the "Admin Dashboard" link in the top navigation bar.

### Step 2: Create a New Exchange
- In the "Create New Exchange" panel:
  - **Event Title**: Enter a name (e.g., "Finance Dept Holiday Exchange").
  - **Target Date**: Select the date of the gift exchange event.
  - **Budget Cap**: The default is set to **K300**. You can adjust this if needed.
- Click **Create Event**.

### Step 3: Add Participants
- Click on the newly created event to open the details view.
- Under the **Participants** tab:
  - Enter the colleague's **Full Name** and **Work Email**.
  - **Important**: In the "Suggestions & Preferences" box, copy and paste the text from the "Suggestions" column of your Google Sheet.
  - Click **Add Participant**.
  - Repeat for all team members.

### Step 4: Draw Names (The Matching Process)
- Once all participants are added, click the **Randomize & Assign** button at the top of the page.
- The system will automatically pair users, ensuring:
  - No one is assigned to themselves.
  - Matches are completely random.
- The status will change to "Active Exchange".

## 2. Participant Instructions (Buying a Gift)

### How do I know who to buy for?
1.  **Access the Portal**: Visit the exchange link provided by the admin.
2.  **Login**: 
    - *Note: In this demo version, click "Employee Login" or navigate to the active exchange.*
    - You will see a screen asking "Who are you?".
    - Click on **Your Name** from the list.
3.  **View Your Match**:
    - Once logged in, you will see a large blue card titled **"Your Assignment"**.
    - The name displayed in large text is the person you are buying a gift for.

### How do I see their Wishlist or Hints?
- On the same **"Your Assignment"** card, look for the section labeled **"Suggestions & Preferences"**.
- Here you will see:
  - The notes pasted from the Google Sheet (e.g., "Likes coffee, dislikes dark chocolate").
  - Any specific wishlist items the user has manually added to their profile.

### Can I add my own Wishlist?
- Yes! On the right side of your dashboard, look for the **"Your Wishlist"** panel.
- Type an item (e.g., "Fun socks", "Notebook") and click the **+** button.
- Your Secret Santa will see these items when they view their assignment.

## 3. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the budget strict?**
A: Yes, please adhere to the **K300** cap to keep things fair for everyone.

**Q: Can I change my match?**
A: No, matches are final once drawn to ensure the integrity of the Secret Santa chain. If there is a serious issue, contact the Admin to reset the entire draw (warning: this reshuffles everyone).

**Q: Is it anonymous?**
A: Yes! You only see who you are buying for. You do not know who is buying for you.
